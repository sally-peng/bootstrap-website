ABOUT US

Frannie ☆ Ariella ☆ Liset ☆ Sally

We are four young women who aspire to make an impact on issues regarding social justice. We cannot stand for the brutality that our legal system has inflicted upon minority groups.

Our mission: To accurately deliver the reality of corruption surrounding the American judicial process. By broadcasting powerful messages from unheard voices and visually representing their stories, we aim to raise awareness for this cause.
